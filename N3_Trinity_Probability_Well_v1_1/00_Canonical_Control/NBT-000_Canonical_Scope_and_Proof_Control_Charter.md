This artifact package validates the finite discrete TPW mathematics only.

Included: finite enumeration, product probability, barycentric/compositional embedding, simplex projection, orbit and symmetry classification, finite contour/ring registries, controlled analogy and correspondence registers.

Excluded from proof status: physical gravity, quantum gravity, universal N-body mechanics, pilot-wave prediction, non-local realism, Theory-of-Everything interpretation, and physical validation of Raphael-side operators.

## 4. Proof-lock acceptance criteria

A claim is proof-locked only when it has a unique claim ID, a unique equation or definition reference, a proof class, a falsification condition where applicable, artifact evidence, citation or derivation support, dimensional status if any physical variable is used, and no uncontrolled cross-domain symbol drift.


All physical correspondence statements remain `P4/P5` unless separately proven, dimensionally audited, and empirically validated.
